OUR COMPANY

Camalig Bank, Inc. (A Rural Bank), formerly the Rural Bank of Camalig (Albay), Inc., started its operations on 6 June 1969. Our founders started the Bank in response to the Central Bank's call to provide credit and financial services in the countryside.

Camalig Bank initially offered credit facilities to the agricultural sector. In later years, Camalig Bank extended its services to include salary, developmental, small and medium enterprise, real estate, microfinance, and DepED APDS (Department of Education Automatic Payroll Deduction Scheme) loans. The changing needs of the communities we serve led to expanding several other products, including financing for gadgets, vehicles, and heavy equipment. These loan types are introduced to meet the business and personal needs of Bicolanos in the hopes of spurring local economic activity.

We take pride in a healthy deposit portfolio deeply rooted in our clients' trust. We have a wide selection of deposit products tailored to help our clients start their financial journey and achieve maximum growth potential for their savings.

Fast forward to fifty years since our humble beginnings, Camalig Bank has gone to great lengths to transform from a single branch to now a solid institution with a region-wide presence through our sixteen branches and five branch-lite offices.

We are a financial institution committed to becoming part of our clients' growth through providing competitive rates and competent service while ensuring good corporate governance and prudent decision-making in all transactions.
